Note the arrangement of household appliances when designing the kitchen

When arranging household appliances in the kitchen, you need to pay attention to the following: Kitchen location is at least 60cm from the sink. The distance between the two cookers is at least 30m so that the handles do not touch each other. Kitchen not arranged near open doors or under windows or large objects The dishwasher should be located near the sink for convenience of use. Avoid placing appliances in the corner of the kitchen. For cupboards and truss doors, it should be easy to open and access, especially when placing them in the corners of the kitchen

The cupboard should be easy to open and access to ensure convenience during use

Kitchen lighting is an important note when designing the kitchen

The kitchen is where a lot of light is needed. Light is crucial to the cooking process and helps you cook better. During the cooking process, you certainly do not want to be influenced by your own shadow. Therefore, install the lighting system in front of the cook. It is best to install the lighting systems right under the kitchen cabinet system to have a light source for cooking.
