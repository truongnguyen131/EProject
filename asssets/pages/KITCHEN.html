SOME NOTES FOR DESIGNING A KITCHEN

Notes when designing the kitchen


The kitchen is considered a place to keep fire, is a peaceful place, a cozy reunion of the whole family. The kitchen has a very important role in the feng shui. For architecture, the kitchen is a complex area with lots of furniture, affecting the basic functions of the beautiful villas and houses. Notes when designing the kitchen, therefore, are also more, requiring the architect to be meticulous and invest a lot of effort in this area.
For each beauty design, if the living room shows the investor' s investment and economic capacity, the kitchen is a place of life and happiness of the family. The ancients used to say, to know what a woman is like, look at their kitchen. But the kitchen can' t show a woman' s wings? The kitchen is warm, the new family will gather, the kitchen is full of interest.

The kitchen needs meticulous computing in each design stage, preferably more utility than aesthetics

Every kitchen, regardless of whether or not, is based on the needs of life, but not based on common standards. Notes when designing the kitchen below is based on general principles when designing for the convenience kitchen, but still carrying aesthetic elements. Notes when designing the architectural kitchen

The kitchen is usually where family members gather, so the kitchen must ensure space for family members. If your home kitchen has a small area, like the kitchen design at the mini apartments, or two - story villas in the street with a narrow area of area, it is best to design the open kitchen and connect with the living room space, which will be more beneficial. For the big kitchens, you should also arrange a kitchen or a bar to have space for members of the conversation while cooking. Such a kitchen represents the warmth of the family, not the complete space of the bedroom.

The kitchen and the living room or the common living room will make the kitchen open as well as the warmth of the kitchen

Note the layout of appliances when designing the kitchen

When you arrange household appliances in the kitchen, you need to pay attention to the following: The kitchen position is at least 60cm. The distance between the two kitchens is at least 100 feet to hold hands. No kitchen layout near open doors or windows or large objects. The dishwasher should be located near the sink for convenience. Avoid placing appliances in the kitchen corner. For the dishes cupboard and the bát door needs to be open and accessible, especially when arranging them at the kitchen corners.

The cupboard must be easy to open and easily to ensure convenience in the use process.

The light in the kitchen is important to design the kitchen

The kitchen is a lot of light. The light decides to make a lot of cooking and help you cook better.In the process of cooking, you certainly don' t want to influence your own shadow. So, install the lighting system in front of the cook .It is best to install lighting systems directly under the kitchen system to have the best light source when cooking.
